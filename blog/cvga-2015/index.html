Lastronaut a Nominated Finalist in 2015 Canadian Videogame Awards

November 17, 2015

Toronto, Canada — Today we're excited to share that Lastronaut has been nominated and is a finalist for Best Indie Game in the 2015 Canadian Videogame Awards!

Since our launch last March, we've been overwhelmed by the amazing response we've seen from the community, and we're thrilled to be recognized on a national stage. Whatever comes of the event on December 6, we're grateful for our amazing friends, family and fans for supporting us along this amazing journey.

Stay tuned by following @lastronautgame, Darrin (@darrinhenein) and Steph (@stephanleroux) on Twitter.

And again, to everyone: thanks!

– Darrin + Steph
